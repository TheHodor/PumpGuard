Terms for guarding a coin at PumpGuard by locking Solana:

- PumpGuard charges total of 0.2 Solana as fee which is taken from the locked solana at the end of the service.

- The minimum lock amount [in total] is 2.5 Solana and any lower amounts will be refunded.

- Any address can deposit Solana to the lock address of a coin but at the end of the service [in case of successful migration] all the balance in the lock address will be sent to the developer address of the coin [dev on pump.fun].

- The minimum lock amount is 2.5 Solana. however there is no limit on how many times developer may add to the locked amount.

- If the coin migrated to raydium the locked solana will be sent back to developer address instantly and automatically.

- If the coin has not migrated to Raydium within 7 days after the lock, the developer may request a refund. However, the refund will only be processed if there have been no rug pulls. Note that if a majority of insiders dump their tokens on investors, this would also be considered a rug pull. In such cases, the refund will be a portion of the locked amount, based on the percentage of tokens that were dumped.

NOTE: The 0.2 Solana fee will only be charged once for a coin, no matter how many deposits were made for it.

NOTE: The minimum lock amount for a coin is 2.5 Solana. A couple of hours after each deposit, if the balance of the lock address is lower than 2.5 SOL, entire balance will be reverted and sent back to the developer of the coin.
