More about our Goal

Undefined Project has a goal of creating a project that is fully investor-driven, where investors can collaboratively shape and define the project's purpose, features and direction, fostering an environment of open-ended possibilities.
This will be possible through our Visionary Voting system [or V.Voting system].
Furthermore, We are dedicated to providing investors with a majority share of the revenue generated by the final developed project [85%], allowing UDF Holders to profit significantly from their contributions, involvement and investment.

Visionary Voting Rewards

The V.Voting system aims to incentivize users to submit well-thought-out ideas by rewarding them with UDF Tokens based on the amount of votes received by their submitted ideas. This contribution-based reward mechanism encourages active participation and fosters the creation of valuable ideas.

UDF Staking

Staking provides an opportunity for Token holders to lock their tokens for a selected duration and receive bonus token rewards. These bonus tokens can be collected immediately. Importantly, UDF tokens will receive a share of the revenue generated by the final project, regardless of whether they are staked or not.

Limits on participating in V.Voting

Each investor has a daily share of voting power, which is calculated based on the amount of UDF Tokens they hold and the duration of the active phase. This calculation involves dividing their UDF Balance by the phase duration in days.

To participate in the V.Voting system, investors are required to hold a specific amount of UDF Tokens [for more details reffer to docs]. Additionally, there is a maximum limit on the number of ideas each user can submit.

UDF Staking, 40%

Staking holds the largest portion of UDF Tokens. Investors can stake their UDF Tokens and earn a daily return based on the duration of their locked stake. The longer the tokens are staked, the higher the return.
Although staking will be available at launch, the primary purpose of the Staking feature is to incentivize investors to hold onto their tokens while the main project, chosen from the V.Voting system, is being built. As a result, we can anticipate a greater emphasis on the staking feature after the completion of V.Voting phases.

Although staking holds the largest percentage of UDF Tokens, it may not be the highest-paying feature. Staking rewards are designed to be distributed over a period of approximately 2 years, whereas Phase rewards will be distributed over a span of 65 days.

The developer of a coin on pump.fun may lock a chosen amount of Solana as anti-rug assurance.

If a coin guarded by PumpGuard fails to reach Raydium due to dumping by the dev or insiders, investors will be refunded with the locked Solana, based on their losses.

By locking solana you can assure your investors about your intentions and future of your coin.

Terms for guarding a coin at PumpGuard by locking Solana:

- PumpGuard charges total of 0.2 Solana as fee which is taken from the locked solana at the end of the service.

- The minimum lock amount [in total] is 2.5 Solana and any lower amounts will be refunded.

- Any address can deposit Solana to the lock address of a coin but at the end of the service [in case of successful migration] all the balance in the lock address will be sent to the developer address of the coin [dev on pump.fun].

- The minimum lock amount is 2.5 Solana. however there is no limit on how many times developer may add to the locked amount.

- If the coin migrated to raydium the locked solana will be sent back to developer address instantly and automatically.

- If the coin has not migrated to Raydium within 7 days after the lock, the developer may request a refund. However, the refund will only be processed if there have been no rug pulls. Note that if a majority of insiders dump their tokens on investors, this would also be considered a rug pull. In such cases, the refund will be a portion of the locked amount, based on the percentage of tokens that were dumped.

NOTE: The 0.2 Solana fee will only be charged once for a coin, no matter how many deposits were made for it.

NOTE: The minimum lock amount for a coin is 2.5 Solana. A couple of hours after each deposit, if the balance of the lock address is lower than 2.5 SOL, entire balance will be reverted and sent back to the developer of the coin.
